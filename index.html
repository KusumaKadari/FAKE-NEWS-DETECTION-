The aim of the fake news project is to help news readers to identify bias and misinformation in news articles in a quick and reliable fashion. We have collected news articles with veracity labels from fact-checking websites and used them to train text classification systems to detect fake from real news. You can paste a piece of text and examine its similarity to our collection of true vs. false news articles, or to news from four different types/genres. Enjoy testing!
